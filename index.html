I have met many web developers in my life, most of them great guys. And while it certainly isn’t true that each developer is a nerd, and struggles with social interactions, I can confirm that communication skills do not belong to greatest strengths of most front-end and back-end web developers. At the end of the day, a typical web developer spends more time with their computer screen than they do with people. And social skills come with practice. As a result, many web developers struggle with job applications and interviews, even with basic questions, such as the (in)famous icebreaker “T ell me about yourself”.
If it is also your case, you landed on the right website. I will show you how to deal with this question.
